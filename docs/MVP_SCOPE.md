# MVP_SCOPE.md

## MVP Goal
Launch a responsive website that proves the core business promise:
JS Auto Parts can generate local customer enquiries through phone and WhatsApp.

## Main Success Path
A local customer lands on the site, identifies the relevant category or enquiry option, then calls or sends a WhatsApp message.

## P0 MVP Includes
- Six responsive pages
- Homepage trust and CTA sections
- Product category browsing
- Product enquiry guidance
- Request Quote form to WhatsApp
- About page
- Contact page
- Mobile sticky Call / WhatsApp bar
- Local SEO content
- Correct fixed business details

## Excluded From MVP
- Ecommerce
- Cart
- Checkout
- Online payment
- Login/account
- Delivery tracking
- Product inventory database
- Admin dashboard
- Fake reviews
- Fake stock
- Fake map

## MVP Decision Rule
Anything that does not help customers call, WhatsApp, ask availability, send a photo, or request a quote is moved to later.
