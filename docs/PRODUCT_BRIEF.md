# PRODUCT_BRIEF.md

## Product Name
JS Auto Parts Responsive Website

## One-Line Summary
A premium dark automotive local business website for JS Auto Parts that helps customers in Khordha quickly call, WhatsApp, ask availability, send a product photo, or request a quote for auto parts and vehicle accessories.

## Target User
Primary users:
- Auto-rickshaw owners
- Auto-rickshaw drivers
- Bike owners
- Scooty owners
- Local mechanics
- Local customers searching for tyres, helmets, oils, batteries, lights, horns, spare parts, and vehicle accessories

## User Problem
Local customers need a fast way to check whether JS Auto Parts has the part, accessory, oil, tyre, helmet, battery, light, or horn they need. They do not need ecommerce checkout. They mostly need to call, message on WhatsApp, ask availability, send a photo, or request a quote.

## Why This Problem Matters
Customers often need parts quickly and may not know the exact product name. A simple enquiry-first website can reduce friction and help them contact JS Auto Parts before visiting the shop.

## Main Promise
JS Auto Parts helps local vehicle owners and mechanics quickly enquire about auto parts and accessories through Call Now, WhatsApp Enquiry, Ask Availability, Send Photo on WhatsApp, and Request Quote actions.

## MVP Goal
Launch a responsive six-page website that builds local trust and generates enquiries through phone and WhatsApp.

## Core Features
1. Homepage with local trust messaging and strong enquiry CTAs.
2. Product Categories page with approved category cards.
3. Products / Product Enquiry page focused on availability and photo-based enquiry.
4. Request Quote page with a simple WhatsApp-based quote form.
5. About page highlighting 25+ years of local trust.
6. Contact page with accurate phone, WhatsApp, address, and hours.

## Out of Scope for MVP
- Shopping cart
- Checkout
- Online payment
- Prices
- Buy Now
- Add to Cart
- Login/account
- Delivery tracking
- Fake stock counts
- Fake reviews
- Fake map unless real map link is confirmed
- Ecommerce wording
- Sunday closed wording
- Mon–Sat only wording

## Platforms
- Website: Yes
- Web app: No
- Android app: No
- Backend: Not required for MVP
- AI: No
- Automation/data/infrastructure: No

## Backend / Data / Offline Needs
MVP can be static/frontend-only.

Required behavior:
- Call Now uses phone link.
- WhatsApp CTAs open WhatsApp with prefilled messages.
- Request Quote form formats user input into a WhatsApp message.
- No database required.
- No backend required.
- No offline-first behavior required.

## Auth / Permissions Needs

- No customer account.
- No admin account.
- No permissions.
